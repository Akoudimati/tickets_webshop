Frequently Asked Questions

To purchase tickets, browse our categories or search for the event you're interested in. Click on the ticket, select the quantity, and proceed to checkout. You can purchase as a guest or register for an account to get a 10% discount.

Refunds are only available if the event is canceled. In that case, you will be automatically refunded. For other circumstances, please contact our customer service team.

If you have an account, you can track your order by logging in and visiting the "My Orders" page. As a guest, you will receive email updates about your order status.
